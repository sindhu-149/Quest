Steps For Conducting Research At first, the step in the Research process is the selection of the topic relevant to the field of research. Like, most researches begin with a general statement of the problem, or the purpose for engaging in the research, after the selection of the topic. Thus, the statement of the problem is a key factor to guide the research in the right direction. In order to conduct a thorough review of the relevant literature, pose appropriate research questions to be addressed. And develop a detailed plan for conducting the study. After the complete review of the relevant materials, specify the general statement of the problem in detail.
